Get To Know

MagInvest is one of the foremost insurance service companies, with a reputation for service efficiency and customer reliability. For over 45 years, MagInvest has honored its underwriting commitments and has earned its reputation of excellence in claims handling. The evolution of MagInvest since 1970 has mirrored the dramatic expansion of indigenous insurance service providers, with MagInvest remaining in the forefront as an insurer of repute. The reputation enjoyed today by MagInvest has been attained by the continuing pursuit of improvements to maintain competitive advantage. All aspects of the business are approached with discipline; the recruitment of staff, the development of products, the advancement of technologies and the personal service offered to each client.

Who Are We

Founded in 1970, MagInvest is the leading composite Insurance Underwriter in Nigeria. Over the years, we have built a solid reputation on Integrity, Prompt Claims Settlement and Innovative Service.

What We Do

We provide efficient financial solutions leveraging on our unique capabilities and skills to bring Insurance as a risk management tool to our clients.
